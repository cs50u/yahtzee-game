How to Play

  1. Click the "Roll Dice" button to start your turn. You have 3 rolls per turn.
  2. After rolling the dice, you can lock and unlock dice by clicking on them. Locked dice will not be rolled in the next roll.
  3. You can choose to roll the dice again or score a category. If you have no rolls left, you must score a category.
  4. Click on an empty cell in the scorecard to score the dice for that category. Each category can only be scored once.
  5. The scoring categories are as follows:
    • Aces - The sum of all 1's rolled.
    • Twos - The sum of all 2's rolled.
    • Threes - The sum of all 3's rolled.
    • Fours - The sum of all 4's rolled.
    • Fives - The sum of all 5's rolled.
    • Sixes - The sum of all 6's rolled.
    • Three of a Kind - The sum of all dice if there are at least 3 dice with the same value.
    • Four of a Kind - The sum of all dice if there are at least 4 dice with the same value.
    • Full House - 25 points if there are 3 dice with one value and 2 dice with another value, or all dice have the same value.
    • Small Straight - 30 points if there are 4 consecutive dice values.
    • Large Straight - 40 points if there are 5 consecutive dice values.
    • Chance - The sum of all dice.
    • Yahtzee - 50 points if all dice have the same value. If you score another Yahtzee, you get an additional 100 points.
  6. The game ends when all categories have been scored. Your total score is the sum of all category scores.
